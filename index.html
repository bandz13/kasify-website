How It Works

Three simple steps to find local businesses

1

Browse & Search

Explore categories or search for specific businesses in your area. Find exactly what you need in seconds.

2

Connect Directly

Message sellers on WhatsApp or view their full details. Get prices, availability, and more info instantly.

3

Support Local

Shop local and empower township businesses. Build stronger communities through direct trade.

List Your Business on Kasify

Business owners can submit a listing that attracts customers with the right look and brand feeling. Share your service, location, and the emotion behind your business so customers get the right first impression.

Why this form matters

The more you describe your business, the better your listing looks to customers. Include your category, location, WhatsApp contact and the feeling behind your brand.

  • Show what makes your business feel modern, friendly, techie, or creative.
  • Give customers a strong first impression before they message you.
  • Use the final custom answer to describe your own brand style.

Quick tip

If your listing is about websites, say it feels techie, modern, and code-based. If you sell beauty, choose warm, friendly, or community-focused.
